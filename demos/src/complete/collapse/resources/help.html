Description

Showing and hiding parts of a graph can be useful for selectively focusing attention on different areas.

With collapsible trees, you enable users to collapse and expand subtrees simply by clicking on their root node. Switch between tree, balloon, hierarchic, organic and orthogonal layouts.

Interaction

Expand/Collapse subtrees

Click a note with a +/- button. Note that there are also leaf nodes that cannot be expanded any further

Select layout style

Choose a new automatic layout style from the drop-down in the toolbar. Choosing a new style, or expanding and collapsing nodes, triggers a refresh of the layout.

Change zoom level

Use toolbar buttons to focus on local areas or to view the complete graph.
