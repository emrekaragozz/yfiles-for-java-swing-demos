Step 9: Edge From Node To Label

Visually connect a node to its label(s) by means of edges. These edges, however, are just visual items and do not respond to user interaction.

Things to Try

  • Take a look at createVisual() and updateVisual() as well as the classes LabelEdgesGroup and LabelEdgeVisual.
