Description

This demo shows how to display different levels of detail depending on the current zoom factor.

In this demo, there are three levels of detail: detail level, intermediate level and overview level

  • In detail level, all of the information is displayed on the node.
  • In intermediate level, some but not all of the node's information is displayed.
  • In overview level, only the most basic information is displayed.

Things to Try

Zoom in and out to see how the displayed amount of information changes.
