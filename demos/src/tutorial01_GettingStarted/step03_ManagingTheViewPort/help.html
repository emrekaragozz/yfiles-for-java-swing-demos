Step 3: Managing the Viewport

The viewport is the rectangle that defines the part of the graph that is currently visible. Its upper left corner is called the view point.

Basic viewport handling is introduced such as zooming with the mouse wheel or the buttons Zoom In, Zoom 1:1 and Zoom Out and making the entire graph visible with Fit Content.

See the sources for details.
