Description

This demo shows the effects of edge and port grouping when arranging graphs with HierarchicLayout. Edge groups lead to edges sharing some of their path segments while port groups will combine the edges only in their first path segment.

Color Codings for Grouping Types:

Source grouped
Royal blue or crimson red,
source port visible.
Target grouped
Dark slate blue or chocolate brown,
target port visible.
Source or target grouped
Cornflower blue or dark orange,
both ports visible.
Ungrouped
Gray,
no ports visible.
