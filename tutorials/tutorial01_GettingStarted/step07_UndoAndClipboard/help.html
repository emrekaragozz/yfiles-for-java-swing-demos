Step 7: Undo and Clipboard

Undo, redo and clipboard support are provided by the graph out-of-the-box.

Undo and redo have to be enabled before they can be used. In contrast, the clipboard is already enabled by default.

If enabled, you can use the commands for undo and redo, and for cut, copy, and paste.

These commands can be triggered by the usual keyboard shortcuts: Ctrl-Z and Ctrl-Y for undo and redo, and Ctrl/Command-X, Ctrl/Command-C, and Ctrl/Command-V for cut, copy and paste. Apart from that you can bind your buttons and menu entries to the commands. This is shown in the GUI section of this step.

See the sources for details.
